Welcome to MovieHub: Your Ultimate Movie Discovery Platform

Are you a movie enthusiast looking for a place to explore and discover new films? Look no further than MovieHub! Our Flask-based web application provides an interactive platform where you can browse, download, and comment on your favorite movies. Whether you're a casual viewer or a dedicated uploader, MovieHub has something for everyone.

Features

User Authentication

MovieHub offers a seamless user authentication process. Users can easily register, log in, and log out of their accounts. This feature ensures that your movie-watching experience is personalized and secure.

Movie Exploration

Dive into the world of cinema with MovieHub's extensive movie database. Browse through various genres, search for specific titles, and discover new favorites. Our intuitive interface makes finding the perfect movie a breeze.

Movie Downloads

As a registered user, you have the privilege to download movies directly from our platform. Enjoy your favorite films anytime, anywhere, even without an internet connection.

Comments

Share your thoughts and opinions on movies with our commenting feature. Engage with other users, read reviews, and join discussions to enhance your movie-watching experience.

Movie Upload

Are you a movie uploader? MovieHub allows you to contribute to our growing database by uploading new movies. Add detailed descriptions, provide ratings, and upload media files to share your collection with the world.

Prerequisites

Before running the MovieHub application, ensure you have the following dependencies installed:

  • Python 3.x
  • Flask
  • SQLite
  • Other required Python libraries (listed in requirements.txt)
